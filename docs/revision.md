# Rental System Workflow

Here is a step-by-step breakdown of the process transcribed from the notes.

### 1. Initial Registration (Tenant)
* Potential Tenant/Inquirer needs to **Register first**.
* Information required:
    * Name
    * Phone #
    * Email Add
    * Age
    * \# of occupants

### 2. Admin Receives Inquiry
* Admin will receive the registration/inquiry.

### 3. Admin Verification
* Admin will **verify** the email by sending an OTP.

### 4. Admin Decision (Accept/Reject)
* Admin can either **accept** or **reject** their registration.
* Must have an "accept" or "reject" button.

#### If Rejected:
* Once the reject button is clicked, the system will send an email informing them that their registration is **denied**.
* The email must include the reason for the rejection.

#### If Accepted (Proceed to Step 5):

### 5. Account Creation (Admin)
* Admin will **create** an **account** for the inquirer first.

### 6. Acceptance Email (System)
* Once "accept" is clicked, the system will send an email confirming their inquiry/registration is **accepted**.
* This email also includes their **email and password** to access the system.

### 7. Tenant Access & Selection
* Potential Tenant can now access their Account to **choose a unit** and proceed **for payment**.

### 8. Tenant Dashboard
* Once logged in, the **dashboard includes**:
    * Available units
    * Unit details
    * Schedule visit button
    * Contract
    * Breakdown of payment (advance deposit)
    * **Payment Section:**
        * Input deposit amount
        * Input advance amount
        * Input rent amount
        * Ref. #
        * QR code
    * **Refund Button:**
        * Requires proof of payment
        * Requires reason for the refund

### 9. Schedule Visit
* Once they schedule a visit, it will be reflected on the admin's side.

### 10. Payment Verification (Admin)
* Once they have paid, the Admin will **verify** the payment.

### 11. Unit Assignment (Admin)
* After verification, the Admin can now **assign** their chosen unit to their name.

### 12. Rent Calculation (System)
* The system will **automatically calculate** their rent for 6 months / 1 year of their stay.

### 13. Final Confirmation (Admin)
* Admin will now send an **email of confirmation** with the following:
    * Confirmation of payment
    * Receipt
    * Copy of contract
    * Unit assigned
    * Reminder for the next due date
